THAKRE ACADEMY — WEBSITE (Floria-inspired editorial dark theme)
================================================================

WHAT'S INSIDE
  thakre-academy-website.html   A single, self-contained webpage.
                                Everything (styles, fonts, logo, scripts)
                                is embedded — no other files needed.

HOW TO USE
  1. Double-click thakre-academy-website.html to open it in any browser.
     (An internet connection is needed the first time, to load the
      React/Babel/icon libraries from their CDN.)
  2. To put it live on your site, upload this one file to your web host
     and point your domain at it — or rename it index.html.

PAGES (single-page app — use the top navigation)

  • Bio & Research — Dr. Amol Kumar Thakre profile + CV placeholders
  • Courses        — Foundation, Two-Year, One-Year pathways
  • Register       — inquiry form (no fees, no exam selection)

PLACEHOLDERS YOU CAN FILL
