🎨 DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS

• Floria-inspired Editorial Dark Theme
  - Warm near-black gallery palette (#0a0e14)
  - Gold (#c8a24a) as the single accent
  - Vignette gradient + minimal structural shadows

• Typography: Spectral (serif) + Hanken Grotesk (sans)
  - Italic-serif accents on key words ("Learn like a researcher.")
  - Computational precision, no bloat

• Zero marketing fluff
  - No student names, testimonials, ranks, or fees
  - Methodology & credentials only

🚀 HOW TO USE

QUICK START (no coding needed):
  1. Extract this zip
  2. Double-click thakre-academy-website.html
  3. Done — it opens in your browser

TO EDIT OR DEPLOY:
  1. Read DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.md
  2. Modify any .jsx files in ui_kits/website/ or tokens/
  3. To rebuild the standalone HTML, use the design system compiler
     (or upload the entire folder to your web host)

DRAG-AND-DROP PLACEHOLDERS:
  • Portrait on the Bio & Research page
  • Book cover on the Home page
  Drop your own images onto these sections to replace placeholders.

CUSTOMIZATION:
  • Colors: tokens/colors.css
  • Typography: tokens/typography.css
  • Spacing/radii: tokens/spacing.css
  • Website pages: ui_kits/website/*.jsx
